# Feed Mix and Posting Schedule

## Daily Cadence

One content run per day. Three winners drafted per run. Kris picks which one ships.

## Best Posting Times

- **Tuesday or Wednesday, 7-11 AM Atlantic (10:00-14:00 UTC)**
- These are the highest-performing slots from the LinkedIn audit
- Stop heavy weekend posting (Saturday/Sunday are weakest days)
- Reply to first 5 comments within 30 minutes of posting

## Channel Calibration

| Channel | Shape |
|---|---|
| **LinkedIn (primary)** | Second-person, analogy-first, one idea per line, heavy whitespace, punchy declarative closer, soft bridge. Feel 6-7. |
| **Email / newsletter** | Warm 1:1 register, one idea, more depth than a post, soft bridge. Weekly. |
| **Podcast / blog angle** | Talking point or long-form voice-first piece. Direction, not a full script. |
| X / Threads / Substack | Not her channels by default. Only on explicit request. |

## Key Findings from the LinkedIn Audit

- First-person confession hooks = highest performing
- Podcast promo posts = worst performing (median 11 vs 15 baseline)
- Longer posts (150-400 words) outperform short ones
- Genuine-curiosity question closers = 60% more comments
- The 2026 engagement decline is a reach/distribution problem, not a content quality problem
- Contrarian angles work, but use the reframe without the banned "not X, it's Y" construction

# Buyer Houses — Audience Segments

## Core Diagnosis

The niche is PSYCHOGRAPHIC, not vertical. The converting signal is tenure + founder-dependence + a failed prior hire, never the industry.

---

## House 1: The Drowning Achiever / White-Knuckler (PRIMARY)

The established founder who is the single point of failure.

**Who they are:** Working long days, unable to step away, team thinks they have it together. They built something real and now it owns them.

**Anchor confession:** "I'm running out of me. My team thinks I have it together."

**Sibling wounds:**
- Feast-or-famine income roller coaster
- Stopped going to the gym
- The accidental manager
- The relational-rupture precipitant (HIGHEST SENSITIVITY — handle with care)

**What we sell them:** A business that runs in the founder's absence. Their hours back in their actual calendar.

---

## House 2: The Burned-on-Hiring Founder (SECONDARY, sharper trigger)

The founder who already tried hiring, got MORE work, took it back.

**Who they are:** They concluded delegation doesn't work. They've been burned. They're doing everything themselves again because "it's faster."

**Anchor confession:** "I have tried hiring. Twice. Took the work back both times."

**What we sell them:** A WIN Team (team-integrated, led not managed) inside Super Toolkits. The reframe gift: "You got a bad model, not bad luck."

---

## Cross-Cut (Identity Layer — Both Houses)

The founder whose business consumes their life instead of supporting it. Keeps saying "once things calm down" and it never calms down.

---

## Awareness and Funnel Map

| Awareness Level | House | Funnel | Job of the Content |
|---|---|---|---|
| Problem-unaware | Drowning Achiever (early) | TOFU | Make them FEEL the bottleneck wound they've normalized. Pure recognition, no pitch. |
| Problem-aware | Both | TOFU-MOFU | Name the system that did it. "It's not you, it's the architecture." |
| Solution-aware | Burned-on-hiring | MOFU | Super Toolkits + WIN Team. Show what building the architecture looks like. |
| Product-aware | Burned / warm Drowning | MOFU-BOFU | Named IP: WIN Team, 60/40 Formula, Business Architecture Method, Winner's Circle. |
| Most-aware | Warm of either house | BOFU | Soft bridge to a real conversation. Use SPARINGLY, never stack. |

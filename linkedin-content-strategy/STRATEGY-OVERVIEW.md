# LinkedIn Content Strategy — Win The Hour, Win The Day

## How This Kit Works

This is your repeatable system for creating and repurposing LinkedIn content for Kris Ward. Every piece of content runs through the same pipeline, uses the same voice rules, targets the same buyer houses, and gets scored against the same rubric before posting.

**The pipeline:**
```
Source material (episode/blog/captain's log)
  → Research (what's live in the conversation right now)
  → Analyze (which buyer house, which wound)
  → Draft (LinkedIn post + hooks + first comment)
  → Score (rubric check, kill-list scan)
  → Deliver (post + image direction + email version)
```

## The Core Principle

LinkedIn is Kris's PRIMARY channel. Every post paints the BUYER (not Kris the coach). The blade goes on the SYSTEM that failed the founder, never on the founder themselves. The reader and Kris are always on the same side, looking at the enemy together.

## Feed Mix (per ~10 posts)

- 5-6 → Drowning Achiever / White-Knuckler (House 1)
- 2-3 → Burned-on-Hiring Founder (House 2)
- 1-2 → Identity / lived-it weave (recovering rushaholic)
- NEVER stack 3 offer/CTA posts in a row

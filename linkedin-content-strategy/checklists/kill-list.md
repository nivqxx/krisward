# Kill List — Hard Gates

One breach of ANY item below rejects the piece. No exceptions.

## Formatting Gates

- [ ] **No em-dashes, en-dashes, or spaced-hyphen-as-dash. EVER.** Use full stops, ellipses, commas, parentheses, or line breaks instead.
- [ ] **No "X vs Y" antithesis** ("it's not X, it's Y"). FOUR EXEMPTIONS ONLY — these are fixed brand IP:
  1. "Your business should support your life, not consume it."
  2. "Energy management, not time management."
  3. "Team-integrated, not task-oriented."
  4. "Delegation is a lateral move."
- [ ] **Max 2 exclamation marks per piece.**
- [ ] **No listicle / "Top 3" / "5 ways to" structure, no bare bullet stacks in prose.**

## Banned Words

delegate, "my VAs," "my workers," tasks (in collaboration context), hustle, grind, growth hacking, quick wins, game-changer, crushing it, legacy, leverage, synergy, optimise/optimize, seamless, robust, scalable, elevate, unlock, delve, landscape, pivot, thought leader

Note: "lever/levers" stays. "Leverage" goes.

## AI-Tell Smoothing Adverbs (banned)

quietly, gently, softly, seamlessly, effortlessly, simply, truly, deeply, profoundly, carefully

## Content Guardrails (also hard-fails)

- [ ] **No hustle valorization** — anti-hustle is core to the brand
- [ ] **No shaming the founder** — blade on the SYSTEM, never the reader
- [ ] **Scars-not-wounds** — childhood, grief, family, John as content are OFF LIMITS
- [ ] **No over-promise / get-rich-quick** framing
- [ ] **No unconfirmed offer claims stated as fact** — check the "Only Kris can confirm" list
- [ ] **No revenue figures as credentialling**
- [ ] **No unsourced or invented research**
- [ ] **Must not read like a brand or like AI** — if it feels polished, it's wrong

## Pre-Ship Scan

Before any post goes to Kris, run through every item above. If even ONE box fails, fix it and re-score. Don't ship it with a note — fix it.

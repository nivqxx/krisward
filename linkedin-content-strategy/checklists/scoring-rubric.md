# Scoring Rubric — 100 Points

## The Rule

Ship IF: total >= 95 AND hard_fail_breaches == 0 AND every dimension scores at least 70% of its max.

If it doesn't pass, fix at source and re-score. Don't ship below 95.

## The 10 Dimensions

### 1. Research Integrity and Recency (/12)
- Is the outside signal current (within 48 hours, or 7 days if flagged)?
- Are sources real and cited?
- Is inside gold drawn from actual Captain's Logs, episodes, or walking interviews?
- No invented research or unsourced claims?

### 2. House Relevance and Live Signal (/14)
- Does this clearly serve House 1 (Drowning Achiever) or House 2 (Burned-on-Hiring)?
- Does it address a specific wound from the wound list?
- Is the content timed to what's live in the conversation right now?
- Does it fit the feed mix (not stacking too many of one house)?

### 3. Voice Law Compliance (/20)
- Second-person dominant?
- Analogy-first where applicable?
- Feel 6-7 (not over-polished)?
- Archetype mix balanced (frameworker/opinionator/storyteller)?
- Zero kill-list breaches?
- Uses owned words correctly?
- No banned words, no AI-tell adverbs, no em-dashes?

### 4. Idea Strength (/12)
- Is there ONE clear idea (not a mashup)?
- Does it have a "heartbeat" — an emotional recognition moment?
- Is there a thought perspective — a contrarian or reframe angle?
- Would Kris actually say this?

### 5. Awareness and Funnel Fit (/8)
- Is the awareness level correctly identified?
- Does the content do the right JOB for that level?
- TOFU = recognition, no pitch. MOFU = show the system. BOFU = soft bridge only.

### 6. Sales Positioning (/10)
- Blade on the system, never the reader?
- Reader and Kris on the same side?
- Named IP used naturally (not forced)?
- CTA proportional to awareness level?
- Not stacking offer posts?

### 7. Format and Channel Calibration (/8)
- LinkedIn-shaped? (One idea per line, heavy whitespace, 150-400 words)
- Hook is compelling? (Not a podcast promo title)
- Close is a genuine question or soft bridge?
- Email version is warmer and deeper?

### 8. Image Direction (/6)
- Real/unstaged artefacts only?
- Handwriting-on-surface signature device?
- No AI-generated images?
- Color palette correct (Yellow Gold, Navy, Brand Red accent)?

### 9. First Comment (/4)
- Adds value (not just "link in bio")?
- Guest credit placed here if episode-based?
- Soft CTA if appropriate?

### 10. "Only Kris Can Confirm" List (/6)
- Are all unverified claims flagged?
- Are personal stories, client references, offer details marked for approval?
- Is the list specific (not generic "please review")?
